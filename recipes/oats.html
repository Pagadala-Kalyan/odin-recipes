Recipe of Oats

Discription

This easy oats breakfast is a creamy blend of rolled oats and milk, sweetened with honey or maple syrup. Topped with fresh banana slices, juicy berries, and crunchy nuts or seeds for added texture. Quick, delicious, and packed with nutrients to fuel your day!

Ingredients

  • Rolled Oats
  • Milk (or plant-based milk)
  • Honey or Maple Syrup
  • Banana
  • Nuts or Seeds (e.g., almonds, chia seeds)

Steps

  1. Cook Oats In a small saucepan, combine 1/2 cup oats with 1 cup milk. Simmer on medium heat for 3-5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  2. Add Sweetener & Flavor: Stir in 1 tsp honey or maple syrup and a pinch of cinnamon (optional).
  3. Add Extras: Mix in 1 tbsp chia seeds (optional) and cook for another 1-2 minutes until the oats are creamy.
  4. Top It Off: Transfer the oats to a bowl and add sliced banana, berries, and nuts or seeds of your choice.
  5. Serve & Enjoy: Drizzle with extra honey or sprinkle cinnamon before eating!
